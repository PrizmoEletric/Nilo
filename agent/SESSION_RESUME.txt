3. SCAN SYSTEM (commands/registry.js + skills/scan.js)
   - "scan [radius]" → scans area, saves to scans/, radius > 2 suppresses chat spam
   - "echo scan [N]" → posts scan N top-20 to chat, one line per message 250ms apart
   - "echo scan N-M" → one summary line per scan in range
